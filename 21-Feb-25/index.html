Common College Incidents

College life comes with various incidents that impact students academically and socially. Exam malpractice and plagiarism are common issues where students cheat or submit copied work. Sometimes, result errors occur due to miscommunication in grades or delayed result announcements. Ragging and protests also take place, with some seniors bullying juniors or students striking for better facilities. During college events, fest mishaps like technical failures, stage accidents, or mismanagement can cause disruptions. In the digital space, cyber issues such as hacking attempts, server crashes, or power outages create challenges. On campus, safety concerns like food poisoning, theft, or fire accidents in hostels are serious matters. Sports injuries often happen when students participate in games and competitions. Additionally, health emergencies arise due to medical issues, stress, or flu outbreaks, affecting student well-being.
